§ 01 · About

A note on the work.

D
D
D

I am a doctoral research scholar at the Indian Institute of Technology Patna, working under Dr. Suman Kumar Maji on deep learning–based image restoration and multimodal learning.

My research focuses on designing and training deep neural architectures from scratch — without reliance on pre-trained models — to tackle low-level vision problems such as image denoising, transform-domain feature analysis, and audio-visual question answering. I am particularly interested in bridging classical signal-processing intuition (wavelets, gradient-domain methods, frequency analysis) with modern transformer-based architectures.

Beyond research, I serve as a teaching assistant at IIT Patna and review for several IEEE and Elsevier journals, including Pattern Recognition and IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics.
